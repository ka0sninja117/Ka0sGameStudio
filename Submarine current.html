SSN Simulator

Objective

Defeat all enemies on the map without dying!

Weapons: (Cycle with "v")

  • MK 48 torpedoes: can fired with or without a target lock (number keys to lock) and will automatically seek out the target with sonar pings.
  • Tomahawk cruise missiles require the player to be not too deep in the water and need a location selected before they can be fired (right click).

    • Intel

  • Get information about the world around you with sonar pings (f) and periscope view (g). Patrol boats stay near islands but enemy submarines will try to ambush you!
  • See what information you've uncovered with the map view (m).

    • Defense & Resupply

  • ADC Mk 5 decoys (c) can help defend you against incoming enemy torpedoes and are fired in clusters of 3.
  • If you are low on ammo or health, call in a Resupply Tender if you are close to an island, stationary, and no enemies are nearby. Tenders can also provide valuable intel.
